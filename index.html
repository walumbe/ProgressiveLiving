Who We are

Progressive Living Wellness Center (PLWC) is a Behavioral Health Outpatient clinic providing therapy to adults who need help to confront barriers that interfere with mental, emotional, physical, and spiritual wellbeing.

Our Services

IOP with Housing

IOP is the acronym that stands for intensive outpatient program. This is a form of addiction treatment that is on an outpatient basis.

Therapy

This is a form of treatment aimed at relieving emotional distress and mental health problems. We offer therapy for individuals, groups and families.

Transitional Housing

Transitional housing refers to a supportive – yet temporary – type of accommodation that is meant to bridge the gap from homelessness to permanent housing by offering support.
